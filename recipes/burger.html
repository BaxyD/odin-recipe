burger

a nice juicy burger to sink your teeth into make sure you have some napkins!

ingredients

  • patty
  • lettuce
  • tomatoe
  • onions
  • egg
  • ketchup
  • mustard
  • mayonaise

steps

  1. place patty on bun
  2. place lettuce, tomato, and onions on patty
  3. place over easy cooked egg on top
  4. apply ketchup, mustard, and mayo to other bun and press both sides together
