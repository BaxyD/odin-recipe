cookie

sweet delicious cookie to round off a wonderful meal or maybe something to bribe your kids with regardless you won't want to miss this!

ingrediants

  • milk
  • water
  • flour
  • eggs
  • sugar
  • chocolate chips
  • m&ms

steps

  1. first get a bowl and place it down
  2. add 1cup water and 1cup milk mix with flour and stir
  3. once mixture begins to set to runny dough add chocolate chips and m$ms continue stirring
  4. once dough reaches a suitable mixture place the dough in individual points on a baking sheet
  5. bake at 350 degrees farenheight for 14 to 16 mins or until cookies have reached desired texture
